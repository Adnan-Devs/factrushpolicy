Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2025

FactRush ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile game FactRush.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect your approximate location to serve more relevant ads via ThinkUp Ads SDK.
  • Device Information: Includes device model, OS version, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized game interaction data (e.g., level completion, high scores) to improve gameplay experience.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Serve personalized ads using the ThinkUp Ads SDK.
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs.
  • Understand user behavior to enhance gameplay experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate ThinkUp Ads SDK to deliver ads within the app. This SDK may collect and use information according to their privacy policy. Please review ThinkUp’s privacy policy here:

https://thinkup.dev/privacy-policy/

4. Data Retention

We retain non-personal data as long as it is necessary to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is stored by us.

5. Children’s Privacy

FactRush is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. User Controls

You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at: support@factrush.app
